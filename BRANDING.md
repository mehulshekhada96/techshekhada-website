## What the app does (for techshekhada.com)

- **SKU extraction & PDF sorting** for **Meesho**, **Snapdeal** and **Amazon** order PDFs.
- Sort order PDFs in seconds; credit-based, secure, built for Indian sellers. Free credits to start.

---

## Features to mention

- **Meesho Sort** – Upload order PDFs, get sorted output; optional CSV/Excel for origin labels; sort by SKU when you skip the sheet.
- **Snapdeal Sort** – Dedicated flow for Snapdeal order PDFs; extract SKU and quantity; sorted output.
- **Amazon Sort** – Dedicated flow for Amazon order PDFs (2–3 pages per order); extract SKU and quantity; sort by single qty, then multi-qty, then multi-SKU.
- **SKU extraction** – Extract SKU and quantity from order/invoice pages.
- **SKU Inventory** – Manage and export SKU inventory data (filters, date range, Excel export).
- **Excel from PDF** – Generate Excel/CSV from PDF data when needed.
- **One-click sorting** – Upload PDFs, get sorted output for Meesho, Snapdeal, Amazon in one place; no manual copy-paste.
- **Save time** – Automate sorting by SKU or origin; focus on packing and shipping.
- **Secure & private** – Files processed securely; credit-based usage.
- **Free trial** – New accounts get free credits; no card required. Refer friends—earn 10% of their first purchase as credits (up to 200).
- **Simple pricing** – Credit packs, no subscriptions; pay per use.
